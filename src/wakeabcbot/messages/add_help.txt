➕ **Adding Items to Watchlist**

**Command:** `/add <keywords>`

**Examples:**
• `/add bourbon` - Watch for bourbon
• `/add buffalo trace` - Watch for Buffalo Trace
• `/add tito's vodka` - Watch for Tito's Vodka

**Tips:**
• Use specific brand names, keywords, or PLU codes for better results
• Categories like "whiskey" or "vodka" work too
• You can add multiple keywords
